GymTracker

The Ultimate Cross-Platform Gym Progress App

Empowering Users to Track, Plan, and Share Their Fitness Journey

Presented by Divij Shrivastava

Date: July 24, 2025

The Problem

  • Gym-goers struggle with manual tracking of manual workouts, PRs, reps, and body metrics using notebooks or basic apps.
  • Data loss is common when switching devices, leading to frustration.
  • Sharing workout plans and progress (e.g., trainers with clients) is inefficient, often relying on emails or screenshots.
  • Existing apps focus on generic fitness but lack seamless plan creation, guided workouts, and social sharing without overwhelming complexity.
  • User feedback highlights needs for simple, offline-first tracking with optional cloud features.

The Solution

GymTracker is a React Native-powered, cross-platform app (iOS & Android) for effortless gym progress tracking.

  • Core Features (Free): Log workouts, track PRs/reps/weights, create custom plans, guided sessions, daily body metrics (weight, fat %).
  • Premium Features (Paid): Cloud sync for backups, share plans/progress with others (ideal for trainers/clients).
  • Offline-first with Firebase backend for seamless sync and real-time sharing.
  • Intuitive UI: Start with a simple home screen for quick exercise entry or plan selection.

Product Demo

Iteration 1 (MVP): Users start workouts, add exercises, view history, create plans, track metrics—all locally.

Iteration 2 (Premium): Unlock cloud backup, plan sharing, and progress feeds.

Competitors & Differentiation

Top gym tracking apps in 2025 include:

  • Nike Training Club: Free workouts but limited custom tracking.
  • Jefit & Fitbod: Strong for logging but no robust sharing or trainer-client features.
  • Strava & Peloton: Social-focused but geared toward running/cycling, not gym-specific.
  • MyFitnessPal: Nutrition-heavy, weak on workout plans.

GymTracker's Edge: Freemium model with offline core + premium social/cloud features; simple UI for beginners; trainer-friendly sharing to tap into the $100B+ personal training market.
